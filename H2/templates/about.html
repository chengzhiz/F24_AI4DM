Chatbot of Truth
Exploring the limitation of language models

Chatbot of Truth is an installation inspired by the Rome tourist spot Mouth of Truth, with the Chatbot as its backbone. The user can ask a yes/ no question and put one hand into the statue. The statue would say yes/no based on its knowledge, and whether it's a proper question to ask chatbot For instance, if the participant asks “Did I have coffee today”, the agent will anser..

  • Milestone2: Advanced version with question generator with different user type
